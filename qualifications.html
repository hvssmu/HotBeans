1) First Qaulification


Proficiency in Web Technologies: Strong skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other relevant web technologies are essential for a web development role. Knowledge of frameworks, libraries, and tools such as React, Angular, or Bootstrap can also be valuable.

2) Second Qualification


Experience with Front-end and Back-end Development: Familiarity with both front-end and back-end development is often desired. This includes understanding front-end frameworks, responsive design, and user experience (UX), as well as server-side languages, databases, and server management.

3)Third Qualification


Problem-solving and Debugging Skills: The ability to identify and resolve issues efficiently is crucial for web developers. Problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and the ability to debug code are highly valued to ensure smooth functioning and optimal performance of websites.

4) Fourth Qualification


Continuous Learning and Adaptability: The web development landscape evolves rapidly, and employers value candidates who show a commitment to continuous learning and keeping up with industry trends. Adaptability to new technologies, frameworks, and programming languages is key to staying competitive in the field.
